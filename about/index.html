About

Ayfa Impex (Pvt) Ltd was founded in 2010 by Engr Zahid Saleem. Since then we have worked hard to provide the best services and products to our customers. With our farms in Bhakkar, Southern Punjab we ensure to provide best quality spices, grains and rice to our customers all over Pakistan and the world.

We not only sell our products in Pakistan but export it all over the world and help our country earn precious foreign revenue and help our economy.

We also import agriculture products like rice machinary, grain marchinary, color sorter machine and other agriculture electrical equipment from China, Germany and UK.
